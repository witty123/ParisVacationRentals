Where to stay in Paris

Paris - The city of Lights..

Situated on the river Seine, Paris is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful and romantic of all cities. Exquisite cuisine, chic fashion stores and priceless artistic treasures characterise Paris - one of the world’s largest metropolis. It comes with a character that’s unbeatable. It boasts of some of the world famous attractions such as Eiffel Tower, Champs-Elysees, Louvre Pyramid, Grand Palais, Notre Dame Cathedral, Versailles Palace, Louvre, Sainte-Chapelle and many others. It’s no surprise that millions of people come to this city every year to discover and re-discover its grandeur and magnificence.

Feel like you are at home

These warm and welcoming apartments are more than just the quintessential place to stay.
